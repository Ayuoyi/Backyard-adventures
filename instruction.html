Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to my lesson?

Bring swimwear, towel, sunscreen, and a change of clothes. We provide all necessary equipment and safety gear.

What if I need to reschedule?

You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your lesson without any fee. Contact us to arrange a new time.

Do you offer private lessons?

Yes! Private lessons are available for individuals or small groups. Contact us for custom scheduling.

What happens in bad weather?

Safety is our priority. If conditions are unsuitable, we'll reschedule your lesson at no additional cost.
