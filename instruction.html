Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our instruction programs

What should I bring to my lesson?

Bring swimwear, towel, sunscreen, and a change of clothes. We provide all necessary equipment and safety gear including life jackets, paddles, and wetsuits when needed.

What if I need to reschedule?

You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your lesson without any fee. Contact us via phone or email to arrange a new time that works for you.

Do you offer private lessons?

Yes! Private lessons are available for individuals or small groups. These provide one-on-one attention and can be customized to your specific needs and schedule.

What happens in bad weather?

Safety is our priority. If conditions are unsuitable, we'll reschedule your lesson at no additional cost. We monitor weather conditions closely and will notify you in advance.
