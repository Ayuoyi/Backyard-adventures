From Childhood Dreams to Reality

Growing up in Jacksonville, brothers Shawn and Harry spent countless hours exploring the local waterways. Their childhood adventures laid the foundation for what would become Backyard Adventures.

After years of corporate careers, they decided to turn their passion for water sports into a business that would help others discover the natural beauty of Jacksonville's waterways.
